election endgame

    Is the election over yet? Not quite.

    election endgame is a miniseries newsletter to help you survive until Nov. 8.

    Each Tuesday and Thursday, you’ll get a dose of news, info about the candidates’ recent travels, a look back over this grueling campaign season and a bonus piece of self-care like a song, video or game. On the week of Election Day, we’ll send you preview emails Monday and Tuesday, and a wrap-up on Wednesday.

    This newsletter is an experiment! We hope you like it. If there is something we can do better, send us an email at electionendgame@dallasnews.com.

    Your privacy is important to us — we will never sell your email address, but we may send you future political newsletters from The Dallas Morning News.
